Frequently asked questions

Is this real?

The hotline is real and staffed by a volunteer network.

Is this affilated with Stack Overflow?

No.

Are you even allowed to call this Stack Overflowed?

We hope so.

What's the catch here?

There isn't! We don't record, store, or sell any personally identifable information about you or what you say.

Why do you need my phone number?

This is the easiest way for us to coordinate which one of us should respond.

Who are you?

We're a volunteer network of people working throughout the tech industry.


Originally, we were a small group of friends who often ranted to each other about work. We believe that humor and candor can help us address (or simply cope) with the unique problems and lifestyle of our industry.

Can I volunteer for the hotline?

Give us a call and mention that you are interested in being a volunteer, at which point we will immediately convert the therapy call into a four-stage technical interview.


Or you can just email us.
